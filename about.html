Our Story

Founded in 2008, the Masala Mahel is truly a vegetarian restaurant for the books, containing many positive critics' reviews and remaining to serve our customers with utmost care and love. We are a family-owned restaurant that believes in the power of food to bring people together.

Every dish is crafted with the absolute most precision and care, using only most fresh ingredients and authentic spices. Our chefs are trained in traditional Indian cooking techniques, ensuring that every bite is a true representation of the rich culinary heritage of India.

100% Vegetarian: Our entire kitchen is meat- and egg-free — always has been, always will be.

Authentic Spices: We only use real Indian spices sourced directly from trusted suppliers in India.

From the Heart: Inspired by family, powered by tradition — everything is made with care.
