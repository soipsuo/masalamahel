Our Origin

Masala Mahel has been running since 2008. The aim is to keep an ambient and peaceful environment for everyone in the restaurant at all times. The goal of this restaurant is to show the delights and joys of Indian cuisine to any individual without care to their background. Indian cuisine can appeal to anyone, and that is what Masala Mahel wishes to showcase.

Every dish is authentic, made with hard work, spices, and most importantly, passion. Made available for everyone to enjoy and experience the joys of Indian cuisine.
